The Best Movies According to Daniel

My top 3 movies of all-time.

"Star Wars: Episode IV - A New Hope"

the best because it introduced us to a galaxy far, far away,
and sparked a cultural phenomenon that continues to this day.

“Star Wars: Episode V - The Empire Strikes Back”

it deepens the characters and plot with unexpected twists and emotional depth,
while expanding the Star Wars universe.

“Fast and Furious 1”

it kick-started the iconic franchise with its unique blend of high-octane car races,
compelling characters, and a storyline about family and loyalty..
